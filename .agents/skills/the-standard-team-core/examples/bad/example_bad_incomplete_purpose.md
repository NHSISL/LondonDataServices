# ❌ Incomplete Purpose Document — Anti-Pattern

The document below violates tst-core-010 by omitting the Future Considerations section.

---

## Business Nature

A bakery is an establishment that produces and sells flour-based food baked in an oven
such as bread, cookies, cakes, pastries, and pies.

## Business Goals

Deliver the highest quality of bakeries to as many customers as possible.

---

*Missing: **Future Considerations** — without this section, engineers have no guidance
on the anticipated growth of the business. They may build a desktop POS system when
the business intends to become nation-wide, requiring a web and mobile platform.*
