# ABC Bakery — Product Purpose

## Business Nature

A bakery is an establishment that produces and sells flour-based food baked in an oven
such as bread, cookies, cakes, pastries, and pies.

## Business Goals

Deliver the highest quality of bakeries to as many customers as possible.

## Future Considerations

Expanding to become a nation-wide bakery business selling across the country.

---

*These three sections allow the engineering team to understand the nature, goals,
and potential growth of the business — ensuring software is built to support that expansion.
For instance, knowing that one goal is nationwide reach helps engineers decide between a
simple desktop POS system and a web application with mobile ordering capability.*
